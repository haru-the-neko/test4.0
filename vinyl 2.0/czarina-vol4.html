For over a year, one reporter has been quietly documenting the small, impossible fact of her.

Feature story: About a girl She didn't arrive with a warning. She arrived the way summer does — all at once, without asking. Every album I've played since feels rearranged around her, as though the running order was always wrong and nobody noticed. This paper is my attempt to set the record straight. It is for her, and only her, and the fact that you're reading it means you stayed long enough to find the back page.
